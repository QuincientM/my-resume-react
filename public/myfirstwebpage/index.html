Knowledge

This is Kyoto, Japan!
Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a city on the island of Honshu.
It's famous for its numerous classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens,
Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses.
It's also known for formal traditions such as kaiseki dining, consisting of multiple courses of precise dishes.

If you want to experience Yamanashi's beautiful landscapes, autumn is the best season to visit.
From October to November, you can enjoy stunning views of valleys adorned with autumn leaves and the breathtaking scenery
of Mt. Fuji and the lakes surrounded by vibrant fall foliage.
